Determinants of Right Wing Populist Vote

Having only a high school degree or lower is the strongest predictor of voting for the AfD

Predictors were estimated in an OLS regression using a representative sample of 1860 voters. A correction interpretation of these results would be to say that, for example, being male is correlated with a 3.8 percentage points higher probability in voting for the AfD holding all other demographic characteristics equal.
Migration background is defined as being born outside Germany or having at least one parent born outside Germany.
Robustness checks using logit transformations yielded qualitatively the same results.
Sampling weights were applied as per the survey documentation.
