All data on voter demographics and voter turnout in German elections are derived from the Year-Year European Social Survey (ESS), an academic semi-annual cross-national survey of over 30 countries. Interviews are conducted face-to-face and include a minimum effective sample size of 1500 for larger countries and 800 for smaller ones.
